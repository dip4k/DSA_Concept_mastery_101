## üß† Why This Approach Works

### The Problem with Traditional Learning
Most DSA courses teach:
- "Here's quicksort. It's O(n log n)."
- "Here's a hash table. O(1) average lookup."
- "Use BFS for shortest paths."

**Students memorize facts but lack intuition.**

### Our Solution: Mental Models
Instead of memorizing, understand:
- **WHY** quicksort is O(n log n) (recursive work at each level)
- **WHY** hash tables are O(1) (collision probability math)
- **WHY** BFS finds shortest paths (explores by distance)

With mental models:
- You can derive solutions under pressure
- You understand trade-offs
- You recognize patterns in unfamiliar problems
- Knowledge lasts **years**, not days
